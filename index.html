About Eva

My work explores the intimate relationship between space and emotion, specializing in atmospheric interior studies and architectural drawings. Educated at Shenkar University, I've developed a distinctive style that combines traditional graphite and charcoal techniques with contemporary perspectives on domestic and urban environments.
